# Product Specifications

## Discount Codes
- **SAVE15**: Applies a 15% discount to the total cart value.
- **FREESHIP**: Applies free express shipping (if selected).
- Only one discount code can be applied at a time.
- Invalid codes should show an error message: "Invalid discount code".

## Shipping
- **Standard Shipping**: Free. Delivery in 5-7 business days.
- **Express Shipping**: Costs $10.00. Delivery in 1-2 business days.
- Shipping cost is added to the cart total.

## Payment
- Supported methods: Credit Card, PayPal.
- Credit Card requires: Number, Expiry, CVV (simulated in this demo).
- PayPal redirects to login (simulated).

## Cart Limits
- Maximum 10 items per unique product.
- Maximum cart total value: $5000.
